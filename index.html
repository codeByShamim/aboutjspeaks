Introducing David Bohm: Executive Director at Apex Chamber of Commerce


As the Executive Director at Apex Chamber of Commerce, David prioritizes staying engaged in the communities where he lives and works. He not only finds meaningful opportunities to help people and organizations but also to encourage those that he helps to find purpose in serving others. David also prides himself on taking nonprofit organizations to higher levels by overseeing and specializing in leading teams, programs and exercises that encourage thought towards innovation, relevance and sustainability in a professional environment.

David is highly organized and driven towards success by focusing on goals. He has a speciality in navigating personalities to arrive at beneficial solutions, supporting a positive culture and creating an enhanced member/customer experience. David challenges everyone to commit to something and to do it with intention, purpose and strive for success in order to overcome the fear of failure and regret!
