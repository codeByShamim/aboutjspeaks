Is Marriage Right For You?


Dr. Murray has appeared in numerous venues, including the “Huffington Post” and “The Daily Mail,” as well as on radio, television, and podcasts such as “The Practice of Being Seen” and “Shrink Rap Radio.” He’s a highly acclaimed presenter at local, regional, and national conferences on various mental health and relationship topics. Dr. Murray has taught at UNC Greensboro, Walden University, and Lindsey Wilson College, and he is currently affiliated with Northwestern University’s Family Institute.


Clinically, he combines a pull-no-punches, no-beating-around-the-bush style with Buddhist philosophy, The Work of Byron Katie, and traditional approaches. This integrated style has helped hundreds quiet the mind-chatter that interferes with happiness, intimacy, and quality sexual relationships.


Last but not least, discover how too much self-control can ruin your sex life and find powerful solutions to help your intimate marriages, relationships, and friendships stay strong.
