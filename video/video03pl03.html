Attachment & Trauma Healing: Building A Culture of Love And Healthy Relationships


It is a pleasure to introduce you to Joy Long, a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC) and holistic psychotherapist. Joy’s intention is to create a liberating and healing space where individuals can discover their authentic selves, tap into their inner resources, and step into their true purpose.


Joy found her calling in this work during the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina. The pain, grief, and anger she experienced while witnessing the suffering of marginalized communities compelled her to pursue a path dedicated to facilitating trauma healing. The guiding principles of Joy's practice as a holistic psychotherapist are love, liberation, and joy. She is passionate about empowering individuals and communities to transform stress and trauma into infinite power and possibility.
