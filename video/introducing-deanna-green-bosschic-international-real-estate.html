Introducing DeAnna Green: The BossCHIC of International Real Estate


DeAnna Green, a remarkable force of nature, is not just your average CEO and Founder of Nydan Group, LLC., a top leading Real Estate Brokerage Firm in Texas. With her distinct accent and larger-than-life personality, she possesses an unparalleled ability to connect with individuals from all walks of life. Despite her diminutive frame, her energy and presence radiate tenfold, captivating audiences and leaving an everlasting impression.


DeAnna, with her magnetic presence and vast knowledge, is a sought-after speaker at esteemed real estate events. Her speaking engagements cover a diverse range of topics, including real estate investment strategies, market trends, entrepreneurship, and leadership. Her captivating presence on stage, combined with her expertise, leaves audiences inspired, informed, and equipped with actionable insights.


Beyond her role as a successful entrepreneur and renowned speaker, DeAnna is deeply committed to mentorship and guiding aspiring professionals in the real estate industry. Her passion for nurturing talent and fostering success has made her a trusted mentor and advisor to countless individuals. With her expertise and unwavering dedication, DeAnna is taking the real estate market by storm. Her innovative approach, backed by years of experience and a track record of success, has propelled The Nydan Group to new heights. Through her dynamic leadership and transformative vision, DeAnna is reshaping the real estate landscape and empowering individuals to achieve their dreams.


In the world of real estate, DeAnna Green and The Nydan Group stand as beacons of excellence and inspiration. With her impactful speaking engagements, personalized services, and trailblazing approach, she continues to leave an indelible mark on the industry, empowering others to thrive and succeed in the dynamic world of real estate.
