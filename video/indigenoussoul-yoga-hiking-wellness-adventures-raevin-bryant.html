Introducing IndigenousSoul Yoga Hiking Wellness + Adventures


I am overjoyed to introduce you to Raevin Bryant and IndigenousSoul Yoga Hiking Wellness + Adventures. Raevin is a certified Wilderness Field Instructor who, in 2017, received formal training through TrailCarolina, a Wilderness Alternative Therapy Program for youth. She has completed over 100 professionally guided hikes, camping, and backpacking trips, with many more to come!


Raevin has been practicing yoga for over 10 years and still considers herself a student. She received her RYT-200 and RYT-500 training and certification from Ganesha's Yoga and Wellness School based in Kernersville, North Carolina.
