Introducing Express & Release Therapeutic Dance


Meet Gabrielle Rivero - CMO and Founder of Express & Release Therapeutic Dance. Gabrielle and her team create a safe space for individuals of all ages to recognize and release emotions through movement. Their mission is to help communities learn to accept and express emotions in a safe way, ultimately allowing individuals to release those emotions. Gabby's vision is to normalize therapeutic dance as a valuable tool for stress relief and coping with lifeâ€™s transitions.


Express & Release empowers individuals to process emotions through movement, fostering hope, healing, and restoration. Research indicates that movement is particularly effective in the senior population, providing numerous benefits, including:


  • Reduces anxiety and depression
  • Improves balance and lowers fall risk
  • Boosts feelings of control and resilience
  • Enhances health outcomes related to dementia
  • Supports health outcomes related to Parkinson's disease
  • Improves blood pressure, bone density, muscle tone, mobility, flexibility, balance, and coordination

Through S.A.F.E. Movement Practice, Gabby guides clients in gentle movements to help them regain mobility and confidence. In her sessions, she invites each individual to explore movements that feel right for their bodies, focusing on sensations, thoughts, and feelings associated with movement.
