Restoring Family Connections


At Bloom Couples Counseling, Ryan offers a comprehensive, scientifically-based approach to relationship counseling in a supportive and appropriately challenging setting. He helps clients unravel the mystery of the cyclical nature of their relationship problems, reducing frustration and restoring connection. Using evidence-based tools and approaches, his goal is to help couples move from distance to addressing problems as a team, leaving both individuals with a sense of pride in each other's core needs and dreams for the relationship.


Ryan is a certified Gottman Therapist and certified Discernment Counselor. He has received advanced training in Emotionally Focused Couples Therapy and is pursuing certification. Ryan works with couples on connection, trust, sexuality, affairs, communication, divorce ambivalence, attachment injuries, and more. He is also trained in EMDR for trauma.


It’s true that a satisfying relationship can be hard to build and maintain over time. But anything of beauty never comes without effort or without help in navigation. Think of Ryan as your mapmaker: he helps partners speak the language of intimacy, connection, and restoration—helping you know where you are and guiding you to where you want to be.
